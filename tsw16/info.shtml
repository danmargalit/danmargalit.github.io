Air

Air travel for the workshop is to Atlanta's Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport (ATL).

Note: To be eigible for reimbursement from the NSF, flights must be purchased from a US-based carrier.

Ground

Workshop participants can travel from the airport to GaTech using the MARTA train. There is one train line leaving the airport. Exit at North Avenue.

Driving: Take I-75/85 to North Ave. Go West on North Ave (right if coming from the north, left if coming from the airport or other points south). Right on Cherry St. Skiles is near the first stop sign. Visitor parking will be on the right, after the stop sign.

Atlanta Lenox Taxi Co (404) 872-2600
